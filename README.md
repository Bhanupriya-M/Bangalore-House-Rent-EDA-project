A quick exploratory analysis of rental housing data to uncover key market trends and patterns.

Key Findings

🧭 Facing Trends
- East-facing homes dominate the market  
- South-facing options are rare  
- A large portion of data has missing facing information  

🛋️ Furnishing Status
- Semi-furnished homes are the most common  
- Furnished homes are moderately available  
- Unfurnished homes are minimal  

⚡ Power Backup
- Most properties either have full backup or none  
- Partial backup is uncommon  
🐾 Pet Policy
- Majority of properties do not allow pets  
- Limited options for pet owners  

🔍 Key Insight
The market strongly favors semi-furnished, east-facing homes with essential amenities, while flexibility in pet policies, rare facings, and partial backup options remains limited
